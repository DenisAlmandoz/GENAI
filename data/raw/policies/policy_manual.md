# Claims Triage Policy

Navigation Menu

## Intake Requirements
Claims analysts must verify the policy number, customer identity, event date, and affected product.
Capture the loss summary in fewer than 90 words.
If the customer shares private information like jane@example.com or 123-45-6789, mask it before storing notes.

## Escalation Rules
Escalate to a senior adjuster when the estimated loss exceeds $25,000, when fraud indicators are present, or when coverage is unclear.
Use a structured output with fields for claim_type, urgency, recommended_next_step, and evidence.

## Customer Communication
Provide a calm tone, avoid legal advice, and cite the policy clause used in the recommendation.
